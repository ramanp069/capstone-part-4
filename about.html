About Us

Welcome to the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) project! This application allows users to explore daily astronomical images provided by NASA. Our mission is to make space exploration more accessible and engaging for everyone.

APOD was initiated by Robert Nemiroff and Jerry Bonnell in 1995 and continues to inspire millions of viewers with stunning space imagery and educational content.

Features

  • Select a specific date to view the corresponding APOD.
  • Explore detailed information, including the title, date, and description of each image.
  • Click images to view them in high definition.
  • Save your favorite images for easy access later.
  • Manage your favorites by removing any unwanted images.

We aim to create an intuitive and educational platform that allows users to deepen their understanding of the universe while enjoying beautiful imagery.
